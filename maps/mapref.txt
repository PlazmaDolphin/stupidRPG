Space = Empty
. = Short Grass
: = Tall Grass
* = Forest
~ = Water
S = Save Block (restores health)
# = Wall

Tiles Specified in map's json:
^<>v = Up, Left, Right, Down Loading Zones (in that order)
0-9 = Interactable Objects, can be tagged and displayed as:
T=Treasure
I=Sign (Info)
P=NPC
M=Monster
D=Locked Door (disappears when opened)

Up -> Down
Left -> Right
Right -> Left
Down -> Up

0 - Treasure (Key)
1 - Sign
2 - Monster (Imp)
3 - NPC (Mom)
4 - Locked Door
5 - Treasure (Sword)
v - To Win Screen
